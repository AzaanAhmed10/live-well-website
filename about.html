About Me

Hello! My name is Azaan Ahmed, and I am the creator of this website. I built this Healthy Life website as part of my learning journey in web development and technology. I enjoy exploring how websites work and learning how to design pages that are both useful and visually appealing.

The idea behind this website is to spread awareness about healthy living and positive lifestyle habits. In today’s busy world, many people forget to take care of their health. I believe that even small changes—like eating better food, exercising regularly, and getting enough sleep—can improve our quality of life.

As someone who is interested in technology and learning new skills, creating this website allowed me to practice HTML, CSS, and basic website design. It helped me understand how to structure web pages, design navigation, and present information clearly.
Besides learning about web development, I also believe in the importance of balance between technology and health. While technology helps us learn and connect, maintaining good physical and mental health is equally important.
My goal for this website is to encourage people to adopt simple healthy habits and become more aware of their lifestyle choices. I hope visitors can find useful information here and feel motivated to make small positive changes in their daily routines.
